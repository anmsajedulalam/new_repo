SELISE Group is a global IT services and product company that specializes in designing, implementing, and operating scalable, cloud-native digital platforms. The organization, founded in Zurich and Dhaka in 2011, uses a "global-local" delivery model, leveraging a worldwide talent pool to provide cost-efficient, enterprise-grade solutions. 
Core Offerings & Expertise
SELISE focuses on a platform-based approach using modular architectures and AI/ML capabilities. 
Proprietary Products:
SELISE Blocks: A composable application platform-as-a-service (aPaaS) that uses prebuilt functional modules ("blocks") to accelerate the development of enterprise solutions.
SELISE Signature: A secure, legally compliant e-signature platform that meets standards like eIDAS and DSGVO, allowing businesses to embed digital signing into their workflows.
Ordermonkey: A platform for the gastronomy industry offering smart POS, omnichannel ordering, and data-driven loyalty programs.
SELISE AdiNova: A comprehensive platform for financial advisors designed to streamline advisory processes and enhance client engagement. 
Key Services:
Consulting & User Experience (UX): Strategic guidance on business processes, system design, and digital transformations, including a dedicated UX Lab for user-centric design.
Application Development: Building high-performing web and mobile applications, often utilizing cloud-native and microservices architectures.
IT Operations & Staff Augmentation: Providing ongoing support, maintenance, and expert talent on demand to supplement in-house teams.
Data & AI Solutions: Implementing AI/ML use cases such as predictive analytics, fraud detection, and recommendation engines.
